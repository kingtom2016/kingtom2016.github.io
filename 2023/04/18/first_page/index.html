Hello

Posted on 2023-04-18 Edited on 2024-01-04

I am Jintao He, a PhD candidate in College of Animal Sciences, Zhejiang University, majoring in Ecological Agriculture under Multidisciplinary Talent Cultivation Program, advised by Yongqi Shao.

I am now working on Ecosystem Microbiome and Stone Heritage Microbiome to better preserve the traditional microbial/cultural resources and to reveal the principle of microbial community assembly.
